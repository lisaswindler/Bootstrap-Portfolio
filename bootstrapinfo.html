About Me

I'm the kind of person who likes to dabble in everything. I love home improvement, fixing things, sewing, making costumes, and any kind of design. I've written two young adult sci-fi/fantasy novels and plan to publish them soon. You can always get me talking about books, ideas, philosophy, and stories. I've always enjoyed learning languages and I have a degree in linguistics.

I've been a stay-at-home mom for ten years while doing freelance projects on the side, such as web design, digital photo restoration, and cutting hair. I just got a new job at a marketing company where I make sure our clients' websites and online ads are running smoothly. Doing this bootcamp is my way of getting a kickstart on my career so I can be a badass and make loads of money while still having time for family and friends because, ultimately, I'm a firm believer that great relationships are the key to a great life.
